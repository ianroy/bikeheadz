02 / 12 THE PROBLEM

AI sculpts the world.
The world is still un-printable.

Image-to-3D AI is real.

Microsoft TRELLIS, NVIDIA Edify, Meshy, Tripo. The models work. Anyone can generate a 3D mesh of anything in 30 seconds.

The output is 3D-printable in theory.

Watertight in practice? Almost never. Open edges, non-manifold vertices, internal voids — you can spin it in a viewer, you cannot hand it to a slicer and get a part.

Bridging that gap is hard, expensive, and bespoke.

CSG booleans crash, repair tools delete geometry, slicers paper over what they can and break on what they can't. Production-grade pipelines are 8 stages of pain.

Result: AI generates beautiful files nobody can manufacture.

The category is gated by mesh-engineering work that doesn't exist as a product.

03 / 12 THE SOLUTION

An AI-to-physical-product
pipeline. Shipping today.

StemDomeZ is the proof: a working e-commerce site that takes a photo and outputs a guaranteed-printable, slicer-ready, watertight, 2-manifold STL. End-to-end in ~60 seconds. The pipeline is the product. The valve cap is the demo.

8-stage CAD pipeline

Normalize · pymeshlab repair · PyMeshFix watertight (v0.1.41) · CSG crop · subtract cavity · union threaded cap · decimate · split-and-process safety net.

Multi-region GPU race

Job submitted to multiple RunPod regions in parallel. First region's worker to pick up wins; loser cancelled. Halves queue-wait variance; one GPU bill per generation.

~60s end-to-end

Photo upload to printable STL on the user's screen. Slider tweaks (head pitch, crop tightness) skip the GPU stage via on-disk cache: ~1–2s.

06 / 12 ARCHITECTURE · TECHNICAL CREDIBILITY

Four tiers. One socket. No REST.

  • Browser — vanilla JS, Three.js viewer, ~50-line History router. No framework. Bundle is small enough to download on a 3G connection.
  • DigitalOcean App Platform — Node 22 + Express + socket.io. Single 'command' event surface. Postgres 18 attached.
  • RunPod Serverless GPU (multi-region) — handler + 8-stage pipeline + TRELLIS. Network volume caches model weights to survive cold starts.
  • GHCR / GHA — image lifecycle. Tag a release, GHA builds, GHCR hosts, RunPod pulls per region.
07 / 12 PIPELINE · THE MOAT

The 8 stages.

Most teams stop at TRELLIS output and ship a viewer-pretty mesh that no slicer accepts. The eight stages between TRELLIS and a printable STL is the product. v0.1.41 added stage 1.7 (PyMeshFix watertight head before CSG booleans) so stage 4's union actually fuses head + cap into a single solid instead of falling back to multi-shell concatenation.

08 / 12 RELIABILITY

Race two regions. Take the winner.

GPU availability is the bottleneck of the AI-to-physical category. By racing two RunPod regions in parallel and cancelling the loser, we halve queue-wait variance without doubling GPU cost. Telemetry surfaces in the operator dashboard at /admin → Regions tab: per-region win-rate doughnut, submits/wins/losses table, last-win latency. Adding a region is one env-var change.
